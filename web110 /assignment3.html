Bing featured a visually engaging homepage with a background image and trending news stories. # DuckDuckGo prioritized user privacy, with it having a minimalist interface while blocking trackers and avoiding personalized search results. # While all three search engines provided relevant information, the ranking of search results varied. Google tended to prioritize well-known websites, while Bing highlighted Microsoft-affiliated content. # DuckDuckGo delivered unbiased results by avoiding personalized algorithms.
